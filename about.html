Daire Feeley

Cycling Coach / Elite Cyclist

About Daire

Daire is a top level cyclist from Roscommon, Ireland. Daire has raced at a Professional and Domestic level and has competed for the Irish National Team on a number of occasions in both European and World Championships. Daire has also won a number of cycling races over the years, he knows what it takes to get one to the top of their game in their sport. Through studies in the area of endurance sports, exercise physiology, coaching and experience first hand of what it takes to become one of the best in the sport of cycling, without doubt Daire has the knowledge and the ability to help you excel in your sport.

“Having competed at a top level in my chosen sport of cycling, and having coached individuals in the sport of cycling, I have learned that there is no ‘One size fits all approach’ , every individual is unique in their needs and goals, and this must reflect in their training approach and the coaching service that they receive. Athletes need a coach who will help them grow and progress, someone who can help keep them on track when obstacles arise, a coach who can provide them with the necessary feedback and advice with their training and other areas that can help them reach their end goal. This is my goal with the service I provide, I provide one with all the necessary tools to help them achieve their goals.”

- Daire Feeley
